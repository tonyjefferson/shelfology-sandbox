Shipping & Returns

Everything you need to know about getting your custom shelving to your door—and what happens if you need to send something back.

TL;DR: The Quick Answers

Does Shelfology offer free shipping? Yes—free economy shipping on orders 24" or shorter and 40 lbs or less to the contiguous U.S. Must be signed into your account. Excludes Alaska, Hawaii, and clearance items.
How long until my order ships? 2-3 days for non-coated hardware, 5-7 days for powder-coated hardware and shelf systems. Check individual product pages for specifics. Shipping takes 2-7 days after that.
Can I return my custom shelving? No—made-to-measure items cannot be returned. Only stock brackets (Aksel, Glenn, Yuri non-coated, Drake) can be returned within 30 days if unused and unopened.
How do I start a return? Email hello@shelfology.com or call 949.244.1083 within 30 days of delivery. You pay return shipping to our Idaho facility.
Can I cancel or change my order? Within 24 hours: free changes or cancellation. Days 2-3: 15% fee (we've already bought materials). After 3 days: too late, no changes possible.
Who pays for return shipping? You do. We refund the product cost only, not original shipping.

Understanding Your Order Timeline

Lead time = how long we take to make your order in our Idaho shop
Shipping time = how long carriers take to deliver it to your door
Total time = both combined

We control lead times. FedEx/UPS control shipping times.

Lead times vary by product—check each product page for specifics. Your lead time clock starts when your order is finalized and paid. If you change your order, the lead time resets.

Need it faster? We charge a 40% rush fee to bump you to the front of the line. Call 949.244.1083.

Important: Faster shipping (2-Day, Overnight) doesn't change our production time—it only speeds up delivery after your order leaves our shop.

Free Shipping

Free economy shipping is available on eligible orders to the contiguous U.S. You must be signed into your account to qualify.

Qualifies for free shipping:

  • Products 24" or less in length
  • Total cart weight 40 lbs or less

Does not qualify:

  • Alaska, Hawaii, or international addresses
  • Clearance items
  • Orders exceeding size/weight limits

Standard Shipping

Most products between 25-80" in length and 1-125 lbs fall into standard shipping. These orders don't qualify for free shipping.

At checkout, choose from:

  • Ground (2-7 days transit)
  • 2-Day Air
  • Overnight
  • International

Rates calculate in real-time based on your order's size, weight, and destination.

We primarily ship via FedEx and UPS (USPS in special cases). No P.O. boxes, but we do ship to freight forwarding services.

Freight Shipping (LTL)

Orders 125 lbs or more ship via LTL freight. Not eligible for free shipping.

We use a broker system to get you the lowest freight rate available. If your shelf is 85" or longer, it ships freight regardless of weight—you'll thank us when it arrives in one gorgeous piece.

Need a custom quote? Call 949.244.1083.

Returns: The Reality of Custom Work

Most of our products are made-to-measure by the inch—sized and colored specifically for you with rad, sweat, and tears from your favorite shelf geeks. Unlike returning a TV to Costco, we can't resell custom items to the next person.

This means you have a bit more responsibility to make sure you're ordering exactly what you want. Please use our resources: detailed product pages, YouTube tutorials, and our insanely talented customer experience team (who honestly geek out on easy transactions and happy customers).

What You Can Return

Stock brackets only:
  • Aksel floating shelf brackets
  • Glenn floating mantel brackets
  • Yuri floating desk brackets (non-powder coated only)
  • Drake floating countertop brackets
Must be:
  • Unopened, unused, undamaged
  • Returned within 30 days of delivery
  • In original, resellable condition

What You Cannot Return

Made-to-measure items:
  • All shelving systems
  • Hooks
  • Cabinet pulls
  • Bookends
  • Poster hangers
  • Swag (t-shirts, hats)

Any altered or customized products (even if the base product is normally returnable)

You pay return shipping. We do not refund original shipping costs.

How to Start a Return

Contact us within 30 days

Email hello@shelfology.com or call 949.244.1083 (M-F, 8am-4pm MT). Have your order number ready and let us know what you're returning and why.

We confirm eligibility

We'll verify your item qualifies for return (stock brackets only, not made-to-measure or altered items).

Pack and ship

Carefully package the item in original, unused condition. You arrange and pay for return shipping to:

Shelfology Returns
156 N. 2nd East
Rexburg, Idaho 83440

Get your refund

We process refunds 1-2 days after receiving your return. Credits appear in your account within 2-7 days depending on your bank or card issuer.

We refund the product cost only—not original shipping charges.

Order Changes & Cancellations

The nature of made-to-measure means that once we've started your order, changes get expensive and complicated. Time and money get wasted. Feelings get hurt. Sandwiches go stale and uneaten. Milk spills. All things that can be avoided.

24-Hour Window

You have 24 hours after placing your order to make changes or cancel for free. Contact us at hello@shelfology.com or 949.244.1083.

If you ordered outside business hours (M-F, 8am-4pm MT), you have until 4pm MT the next business day.

Have ready: Order number and contact information

Days 2-3: The Material Zone

We've already purchased materials and started cutting. Changes or cancellations during this window incur:

  • 15% time/material fee on entire order
  • Plus any additional costs specific to your request

Payment for changes must be received before we resume work.

After 3 Business Days: Too Late

Your order is too far along. No changes, edits, or cancellations possible. All sales are final.

Timeframe Fee What's Possible
0-24 hours Free Changes and cancellations allowed
Days 2-3 15% fee + additional costs Changes possible with fees
After day 3 N/A No changes allowed
Note: If you've ordered an altered version of our products (changed from website configuration), sales are final after 24 hours regardless.

Defects & Warranty

If something arrives with a manufacturing defect, we'll repair or replace it. Warranty coverage varies by product—check our Warranty Page for specifics.

For damaged items requiring repair or replacement, contact us at 949.244.1083 or hello@shelfology.com. We'll send you a return label if needed.

We are not responsible for items lost, stolen, or damaged in transit. If your package goes full "Roddy Piper" after leaving our facility or disappears from your porch, file a claim with the carrier using your tracking number.

Custom & Altered Orders

Custom order: A one-off product not available on our website, made just for you (ex: a custom towel bar).

Altered order: A product from our website with modifications (ex: changing an Aksel shelf from 13" to 13.5").

We offer custom and altered orders to our Trade Army (professional customers). If you're a trade pro, sign up and contact your rep at 949.244.1083 or sales@shelfology.com.

For non-Trade customers

We offer limited alterations on existing products:

  • Length
  • Depth
  • Rod count
  • Rod length
Important: Once altered, items cannot be returned or cancelled after 24 hours (manufacturing defects still covered under warranty).

Promotions
